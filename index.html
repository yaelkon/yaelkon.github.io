My research focuses on **algorithmic bias mitigation** in AI systems using **explainable AI (XAI)** methods — building tools that not only explain model decisions, but leverage those explanations to make AI fairer and more trustworthy. Before Cambridge, I spent five years as a Research Scientist at SightX AI, designing and deploying real-time deep learning systems for aerial object detection on edge hardware. I hold an MSc with Distinction (GPA 96/100) from Ben-Gurion University, where I developed probabilistic methods for interpreting deep neural networks.
